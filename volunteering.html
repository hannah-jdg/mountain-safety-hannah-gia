Why Volunteer with Us?

Kalmont hosts a variety of informative sessions designed to keep our community engaged and educated. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned adventurer, our sessions offer valuable insights. Join us for the Hypothermia Awareness Workshop, where experts will discuss prevention, symptoms, and treatment in cold-weather environments, or attend our Mountain Safety Information Session to learn essential skills for navigating the rugged terrain safely. For those interested in environmental conservation, we offer the Sustainable Practices Seminar, focusing on eco-friendly hiking and preserving the natural beauty of Kalmont's surroundings. These sessions are a great opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals and gain the knowledge needed to stay safe and responsible while enjoying the great outdoors.
