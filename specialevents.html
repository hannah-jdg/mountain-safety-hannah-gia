Our Special Events

Kalmont is a hub of exciting events year-round, offering something for everyone, from outdoor enthusiasts to culture lovers. Adventure seekers can participate in the Valmont Mountain Challenge, an endurance race through rugged trails, or enjoy the Sunset Paddle Fest, where kayakers and paddleboarders take to the lake under a glowing sky. Those looking for a more relaxed experience can explore the Valmont Artisan Market, featuring handcrafted goods and local delicacies, or attend the Music in the Park series, where live performances fill the summer air.

Featured Event: Kalmont Night Ops

Join us for a night rescue simulation made for volunteers using search-and-rescue tactics in a controlled outdoor environment.

Social Events

Celebrate and connect with the community through lively gatherings, from après-ski parties to outdoor dining experiences.

First-aid & Rescue Events

Learn essential survival skills and emergency response techniques through hands-on workshops and live demonstrations.

Fundraiser Events

Support important causes while enjoying exciting activities, from charity races to gala dinners that give back to the community.
