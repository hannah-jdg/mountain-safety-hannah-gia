Our Story

For over 30 years, Kalmont Mountain Rescue has been dedicated to keeping adventurers safe in the rugged terrain of the Kalmont Mountain Range. Whether you're hiking, climbing, or backcountry skiing, we provide real-time updates, expert rescue services, and essential safety resources to ensure your journey is both thrilling and secure.

Our team consists of highly trained rescue professionals, paramedics, and volunteers who specialize in rapid response, search and rescue operations, and wilderness first aid.

At Kalmont Mountain Rescue, our mission is to keep adventurers safe through rapid response, expert rescue operations, and up-to-date safety information. Whether preventing accidents or providing emergency assistance, we are dedicated to protecting everyone who explores Kalmont’s mountains.
