About Risk Levels & Status

Mountain risk levels help assess the potential dangers in alpine environments, including avalanche hazards, weather conditions, and trail safety. These levels provide critical guidance for hikers, skiers, and climbers, helping them make informed decisions before heading out. Always check the latest risk updates and plan accordingly to ensure a safe adventure.
