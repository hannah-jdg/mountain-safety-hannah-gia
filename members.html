Meet the Kalmont Teams & Members

Kalmont Mountain Rescue is a team of skilled professionals and dedicated volunteers, working year-round to keep our mountains safe.

Who We Are

Kalmont Mountain Rescue is a dedicated team of highly trained rescuers, medics, and volunteers committed to keeping our mountain community safe. With experience in search and rescue, avalanche response, and wilderness first aid, we work around the clock to provide assistance when it’s needed most.

Why We Do It

We believe that everyone should be able to explore the mountains safely. Whether you’re a local, a visitor, or a seasoned adventurer, our goal is to provide the support, information, and rescue services needed to keep Kalmont’s slopes and trails safe for all.

From skilled mountaineers to medical professionals, our team brings together a diverse range of expertise to handle emergencies in extreme conditions. We train regularly in search techniques, rope rescues, and survival strategies to ensure fast and effective response times.

Volunteer Rescuers & Specialists

Our rescue efforts rely on a network of trained volunteers, including:

Ski Patrollers – First responders on the slopes

Climbing Experts – Technical rope rescue specialists

Search Dog Handlers – Teams trained in K9 search & rescue

Wilderness Medics – Emergency responders trained in remote medical aid
