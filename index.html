WE ARE RESCUERS

Committed to keeping you informed and safe on the mountain so you can enjoy the slopes.

We keep people safe in the mountains.

For over 30 years, Kalmont Mountain Rescue has been dedicated to keeping adventurers safe in the rugged terrain of the Kalmont Mountain Range. Whether you're hiking, climbing, or backcountry skiing, we provide real-time updates, expert rescue services, and essential safety resources to ensure your journey is both thrilling and secure.

Our team consists of highly trained rescue professionals, paramedics, and volunteers who specialize in rapid response, search and rescue operations, and wilderness first aid.

Want to get involved?

At Kalmont, we believe in creating unforgettable experiences that bring people together and celebrate our community’s spirit. From event planning and logistics to marketing and guest services, every role plays a crucial part in making Valmont’s events a success.

If you’re looking for a dynamic and rewarding opportunity to be part of something special, we’d love to have you on our team.
